## Components

**MainHall** - Central hall serving as the main gathering space and hub of the palace.

**ThroneRoom** - Royal throne room for ceremonies and audiences.

**Kitchen** - Food preparation area with cooking facilities.

**Bedroom** - Royal sleeping quarters.

**Treasury** - Secure vault for valuables and treasures.

**Barracks** - Guard quarters and training area.

**Garden** - Decorative outdoor garden space.

**Wall** - Protective wall structure.

**GateHouse** - Entrance gatehouse with defensive features.

## Constraints

ADJACENT(MainHall, ThroneRoom, n)
ADJACENT(MainHall, Kitchen, w)
ADJACENT(MainHall, Bedroom, e)
ADJACENT(MainHall, Barracks, s)
ADJACENT(Bedroom, Treasury, e)
ADJACENT(Wall, MainHall, a)
ADJACENT(Wall, Garden, a)
ADJACENT(Wall, GateHouse, s)
ADJACENT(Garden, MainHall, w)
